# MAPLOV CANADA

## Mobile Dating Application Proposal

### Overview
MapLov Canada is a bilingual mobile dating application designed specifically for people living in Canada who want to build meaningful social and romantic connections.

The application combines modern matchmaking features with geolocation technology, messaging, and community interaction to help users connect with compatible people nearby.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people in Canada face challenges when trying to meet compatible partners and build meaningful relationships.

Some common challenges include:

- Difficulty meeting new people due to busy lifestyles
- Limited opportunities for community interaction
- Lack of bilingual (English/French) dating platforms
- Challenges faced by newcomers and immigrants when building social connections

---

## Target Users

MapLov Canada is designed for:

- Adults 18 years and older
- Students
- Young professionals
- New immigrants
- Individuals looking for friendship, dating, or serious relationships

---
