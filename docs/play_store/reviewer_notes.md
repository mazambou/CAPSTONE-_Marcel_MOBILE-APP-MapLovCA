# Google Play reviewer access notes — draft

MapLov is an adults-only dating and social application. A reviewer account must be supplied in Play Console after final test users are created.

Suggested review path:

1. Sign in with the supplied reviewer account.
2. Open Discover and Filters.
3. Open Likes, Matches and Messages from the bottom navigation.
4. Open Profile to manage public photos and photo display style.
5. Open Settings → Privacy, Legal & consent, Blocked Users and Delete Account.
6. Use the overflow menu on a profile or conversation to access separate Report and Block controls.
7. Open Friends posts to report user-generated content.

The Secret Garden contains private, time-limited albums. Reviewer test data must avoid real intimate or sensitive imagery.
